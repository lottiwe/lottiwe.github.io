Our Services

At Indis Fishery, we offer a wide range of services designed to cater to your fishing needs:

1. Catering for Events

We provide fresh fish and seafood catering for events, ensuring that your guests enjoy the best quality dishes made from our sustainably sourced seafood.

2. Fishing Tours

Join us for an unforgettable fishing experience! Our guided tours take you to the best fishing spots, with expert tips and equipment provided.

3. Fish Processing Services

We offer fish cleaning, filleting, and packaging services to make your catch ready for cooking or storage.

4. Seafood Cooking Classes

Learn how to prepare delicious seafood dishes with our hands-on cooking classes, perfect for chefs of all skill levels.

5. Wholesale Seafood Supply

We supply fresh fish and seafood to restaurants and businesses at competitive prices, ensuring quality and freshness in every delivery.

6. Aquaculture Consulting

Our team of experts provides consulting services for aquaculture operations, helping you optimize production and sustainability.

7. Customized Fishing Packages

We offer personalized fishing packages tailored to your preferences, whether for a family outing or a corporate event.

8. Community Education Programs

Engaging programs aimed at educating the community about sustainable fishing practices and marine conservation efforts.

9. Recreational Fishing License Assistance

We provide guidance and assistance in obtaining your recreational fishing licenses, making your fishing trips hassle-free.

10. Eco-Tours

Explore the beauty of marine ecosystems with our eco-tours, designed to promote awareness and appreciation of marine life.
