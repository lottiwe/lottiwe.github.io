Our Services

At Indis Fishery, we offer a wide range of services designed to cater to your fishing needs:

1. Catering for Events

We provide fresh fish and seafood catering for parties, weddings, and corporate events. Our team will ensure a delightful experience for you and your guests.

2. Boat Rentals

Enjoy a day on the water with our boat rental service. Perfect for fishing trips or leisurely outings with family and friends.

3. Fish Market

Visit our fish market open from 6 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday, and 8 AM to 4 PM on Saturdays. We offer the freshest catch of the day!

4. Guided Fishing Tours

Join us for guided fishing tours led by experienced professionals. We provide all the necessary equipment and expertise for a great fishing experience.

5. Customized Packages

We can create customized packages tailored to your specific needs, whether for a special occasion or regular outings.
